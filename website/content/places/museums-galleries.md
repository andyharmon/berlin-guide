## Mitte

### [Samurai Museum](geo:52.52697258264584,13.395316257592714)tag:museum

Museum about the history of the samurai. Apparently the only one in Europe... Weird it's in Berlin.

### [Deutsches Historisches Museum](geo:52.51876354811879,13.396302729740128)tag:museum

Houses exhibitions on German History, including German natural history.

### [Tränenpalast](geo:52.521054422700225,13.387239121909413)tag:museum

An old crossing point which now houses a permanent exhibition on the lives of people in divided Germany.

### [Museum für Kommunikation](geo:52.510114974605216,13.387318839152687)tag:museum

Museum on communication technologies and the German postal system.

### Museumsinsel

Museum Island is _technically_ an island on the Spree built in the 1800s. The museums on the island focus on archeology and the art of the 19th century.

Each museum charges a separate entry but if you want to do them all, you can get multiple admissions for a discount.

#### [Pergamonmuseum](geo:52.5212526320652,13.396864873124516)tag:museum

> [!warning]
> The Pergamonmusem is closed until 2017 due to construction work. You can find out more about the works on the [website](https://www.smb.museum/en/museums-institutions/pergamonmuseum/home/).

This is the last built museum on Museuminsel and houses the great acquisitions of German archaeologists in the 19th and 18th century. The museum has the Pergamonsaal and alter. It is very similar in scale and character to the British museum.

#### [Neues Museum](geo:52.52029810744049,13.397758852643964)tag:museum

A museum containing collections of Egyptian and prehistoric archaeology. It houses the famous bust of Nefertiti (the legality of its acquisition is still contested by the Egyptian state which is trying to get it back!) Been here.

#### [Altes Museum](geo:52.519597084569014,13.39869623282921)tag:museum

A museum containing displays of Roman and Greek artefacts.

#### [Alte Nationalgalerie](geo:52.52094749218223,13.398379721972661)tag:gallery

19th-century painting and sculpture with many famous and important artists represented.

#### [Bode-Museum](geo:52.52214480058291,13.394328562452813)tag:gallery

Gallery of sculpture and Byzantine art. Some collections of art from the middle-ages.

#### [Stadtschloss and the Humboldt Forum](geo:52.51729205939235,13.401703006390079)tag:museum

A former residence of electors, kings and emperors which is now a museum. The original palace was badly damaged during the second world war and was replaced by the GDR with the Palast der Republik. This was dismantled during reunification when the GDR parliament became obsolete. The building here is a reconstruction of the monarchist palace.

### [Hanf Museum](geo:52.516729978364936,13.40813890659095)tag:museum

Museum about the biology and history of hemp and it's uses. Quite a small museum.

### [DDR Museum](geo:52.519381868339025,13.402685359225229)tag:museum

A really relaxed and interactive museum about everyday life of people in the DDR. It's a bit expensive for what it is but it's a really comprehensive look at how people lived under the Soviets.

### [Haus der Kulturen der Welt](geo:52.51866212786648,13.364502450750955)tag:museum

A world museum of contemporary, non-European art and culture.

### [Deutschlandmuseum](geo:52.50904750992432,13.378543826651912)tag:museum

An interactive museum of the history of Germany. Geared more towards families but can be a laugh as an adult.

### [Kunstgewerbemuseum _Museum of Decorative Arts_](geo:52.50979915345124,13.366927928489433)tag:museum

Collection of European applied arts and design.

### [Gemäldegalerie _Old Master Paintings_](geo:52.50878960773459,13.365301741341815)tag:gallery

A collection of 13th-18th-century paintings in a modern setting.

### [Neue Nationalgalerie](geo:52.50734139834655,13.36782300557479)tag:gallery

Modern art gallery of European and American pieces.

## Charlottenburg, Wilmersdorf and Schöneberg

### [Museum für Fotografie](geo:52.50839628173291,13.331890749005478)tag:gallery

Museum of photography with rotating exhibitions.

### [Bröhan Museum](geo:52.51901515520895,13.295315777778312)tag:gallery

A collection of Art Nouveau and Art Deco pieces.

### [Scharf-Gerstenberg Collection](geo:52.51928706974604,13.29658150473697)tag:gallery

A gallery of paintings and sculptures from the surrealist movement.

### [Hamburger Bahnhof - Nationalgalerie der Gegenwart](geo:52.52846511453012,13.37212090658626)tag:gallery

Gallery of contemporary art showing works from the 1960s onward. It is named from the building being the former Hamburger train station.

## East Central (Kreuzberg, Friedrichshain, Prenzlauer Berg)

### [Dokumentationszentrum Topographie des Terrors, _Topography of Terror_](geo:52.50689719661697,13.383661620124142)tag:museum

On the site of the former Gestapo headquarters, a museum documenting the horrors of Nazism. Free.

### [Jüdisches Museum](geo:52.50248130458078,13.395361069349281)tag:museum

Museum of Jewish life and history in Berlin and Germany. Contains a _haunting_ Holocaust memorial.

### [Deutsches Technikmuseum, _German Museum of Technology_](geo:52.49885495559647,13.377873871204372)tag:museum

Huge museum to German technology and engineering, covering a vast range of topics.

### [Computerspielmuseum](geo:52.51758068785906,13.441911584689132)tag:museum

Computer game museum with some classics and rare originals. Includes some games from East Germany.

### [Museum at the Kulturbrauerei](geo:52.540565708036674,13.414225746098962)tag:museum

Museum of the lives of people in the DDR and East Berlin. A really good introduction to the ethos of the country and it's ultimate demise. Provides a free audio guide. Free.

## East (Lichtenberg, Marzahn-Hellersdorf and Pankow)

### [Stasi Museum](geo:52.51460919058245,13.487240146670304)tag:museum

A museum describing the tactics of the Stasi, the secret police in East Germany.

### [Karlshorst Museum](geo:52.48651358787686,13.539659883413337)tag:museum

Museum documenting the war on the Eastern front between G---ermany and the USSR and the relationship between the GDR/Germany and the USSR/Russia since.
