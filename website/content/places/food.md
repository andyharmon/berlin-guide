## Dinner

### [Ora](geo:52.50144067311083,13.415846598192637)tag:food

Notionally a wine bar but serves seasonal European food. Offers a ~€60 Chef's Choice menu but you can pick and choose. Gothic setting Sample menu is mostly vegetarian.
[Website](https://ora.berlin)

### [PeterPaul](geo:52.53003814158253,13.404341001890414)tag:food

A stylish restaurant serving typically German food on small plates. Some good vegan options. Not cheap
[Website](http://peterpaul.berlin/)

### [Trio](geo:52.52697819143163,13.414680044232902)tag:food

Neighbourhood restaurant serving food inspired from Central Europe for lunch and dinner. Menu changes every week. Not an awful lot of vegetarian or vegan options here. Been here.
[Website](https://www.trioberlin.net)

### [Niko Niko Ramen](geo:52.51279921185819,13.458000786538628)tag:food

Straightforward noodles and dumplings, at a decent price. All noodles are vegan so plenty of vegetarian options here.

### [Sathutu](geo:52.537227048206134,13.421471199466028)tag:food

Sri Lankan food made with local produce. Most dishes can be made plant-based. Open Wednesday-Saturday.

### [Café Pilz](geo:52.47879257757303,13.42382115765304)tag:food

Relaxed Levantine restaurant with a vegetarian and mostly vegan menu. Does a brunch menu Thursday-Sunday.

### [Dotori](geo:52.55342212912923,13.433595877764835)tag:food

A Korean [Anju](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anju_(food)>) bar. Food is a complete surprise as is custom. Everyone shares and get drunk. You an chose from a vegan, vegetarian or meat menu.

### [Mater](geo:52.485828812741524,13.435268402503898)tag:food

Pizza restaurant serving different pizzas and Italian dishes. Serves vegan pizzas _with vegan mozzarella_.
